What is a stand? A Stand (スタンド Sutando) is the physical manifestation of an invididual's life energy, named such because they typically appear beside their user. Who can use a stand? Stand capabilities can be developed naturally or acquired through the use of ancient arrows made from meteoric material. Using an arrow requires immense spiritual strength to awaken a stand successfully. Who can see stands? In most cases, stands are only visible to other stand users. However, stand abilities can affect non-stand users.
